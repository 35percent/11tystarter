---
title: Aylesbury Phase 2B - Notting Hill Genesis try again
description: Second attempt to make progress on stalled Aylesbury regeneration
date: 2024-11-09T11:12:27.174Z
---
Notting Hill Genesis have updated their planning application for Phase 2B of the Aylesbury regeneration.  The new proposals increase the number of homes from 617 to 640, with an increase in the number of social rented homes from 163 to 173 and of intermediate part-buy/part-let homes from 82 to 89.  The number of private homes has been increased to 378 from 369.
